The NEXUS
Member Code.

The Code turns our values into shared standards of conduct. It applies online, offline, in teams, learning programs, events, and public projects.

Applies to everyone10 principlesVersion 1.0 / 2026
Member pledge
I develop myself, help others grow, and direct my knowledge toward creation and positive impact.
01

Ten principles

These principles define not a member’s status, but the quality of their actions and relationship with the community.

Every person can change

We do not define people by past mistakes. We value their willingness to learn, take responsibility, and move forward.

All honest work deserves respect

Profession, income, and status do not define dignity. What matters is honest work and the value it creates.

Impact begins with self-development

Members develop their knowledge, well-being, resilience, and professionalism so they can support others.

Created value opens opportunities

We create value first, then earn trust, relationships, recognition, and new opportunities.

Success is built through daily action

We value discipline, consistency, and completed results. Mistakes are acceptable; repeated irresponsibility is not.

We support the community

Members protect NEXUS, share feedback, contribute to common goals, and never use the community solely for personal gain.

Strong people help others grow stronger

We share experience without arrogance and help others become independent rather than dependent.

We unite rather than divide

Discrimination, bullying, humiliation, and hate speech are incompatible with NEXUS.

Knowledge becomes action

Learning should lead to a task, project, research, support for another person, or measurable improvement.

Development never ends

Achievement is not a reason to stop. Every member remains a learner and stays open to constructive feedback.

02

How we work together

The minimum standard for every NEXUS team and interaction.

◌

We listen

We clarify meaning, distinguish facts from assumptions, and make room for different perspectives.

✓

We deliver

We document agreements, meet deadlines, and communicate early when commitments change.

↔

We speak directly

We resolve conflict with those involved, without public pressure, rumors, or indirect attacks.

↑

We empower

We give specific feedback, recognize team contributions, and share useful knowledge.

Freedom to participate does not remove responsibility.

By joining NEXUS, every person accepts this Code and helps maintain a safe, honest, and constructive environment.
