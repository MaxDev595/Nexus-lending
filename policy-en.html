A safe environment / 03

Community
Policy.

This Policy explains how NEXUS supports equal opportunity, safe interaction, and fair review of difficult situations.

For members and partnersApplies in every spaceVersion 1.0 / 2026
Our position
Who a person is today does not define them. What matters is the desire to improve and the willingness to respect others.
01

Shared environment standards

This Policy applies to events, platforms, chats, projects, programs, and communication on behalf of NEXUS.

01

Equality and accessibility

NEXUS welcomes people regardless of origin, nationality, gender, age, profession, wealth, social standing, or life experience. Selection may consider only program requirements and willingness to grow and follow the rules.

02

Respect and safety

Bullying, threats, harassment, discrimination, humiliation, sexual pressure, hate speech, and sharing personal information without consent are prohibited.

03

Voluntary participation

Members choose their own paths, roles, and projects. No one may demand unconditional obedience or use NEXUS authority as leverage.

04

Integrity and conflicts of interest

Members disclose personal interests that could affect decisions. Fraud, plagiarism, manipulation, and hidden personal use of resources are prohibited.

05

Privacy and data

Personal information, internal materials, and unpublished ideas are used only for agreed purposes. Never share another person’s data, photos, or messages without permission.

06

Intellectual contributions

Authorship must be credited honestly. Terms for collaborative work are agreed before publication or commercial use.

07

Public communication

Personal opinions are not the official position of NEXUS. Only authorized representatives may speak for the organization.

02

When a rule is broken

The process aims to stop harm, restore safety, and assess the situation fairly.

A

Report

Report a violation to a coordinator or community team with available facts and evidence. Reports are handled as confidentially as possible.

B

Review

A responsible representative hears all parties, verifies facts, and avoids conflicts of interest.

C

Decision

Measures may include guidance, a warning, restorative action, access restrictions, suspension, or removal.

D

Appeal

A review may be requested if significant new facts emerge or the procedure was not followed.

A strong community begins with personal responsibility.

NEXUS provides an environment, knowledge, and opportunities. Only the individual can turn opportunity into results.
